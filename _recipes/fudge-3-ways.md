## Ingredients
### Classic
- 3 cups sugar
- 2/3 cups cocoa
- 1 1/2 cup milk
- 4 1/2 Tbsp butter
- 1/8 tsp salt
- Vanilla
- Peanut butter

### White
- 3 cups sugar
- 1 cup half and half
- 2 Tbsp white karo
- 2 Tbsp oleo
- 1 Tbsp butter (for after boiling)
- 1 tsp vanilla
- Salt
- (Optional) nuts

### Marshmallow
- 4 cups sugar
- 1 stick oleo
- 1 large can milk
- 1 pint marshmallow cream
- 6 oz chocolate chips
- 6 oz nuts OR 6 oz mint chips


## Directions
1. Boil the sugar, milk, cocoa, and butter type ingredients to soft ball stage
2. Allow to cool slightly and then beat in remaining ingredients
