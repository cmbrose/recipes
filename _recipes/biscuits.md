## Ingredients
- 2 cups flour
- 4 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp cream of tartar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 Tbsp sugar
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1 egg
- 2/3 cup milk

2. Mix ingredients
3. Roll out dough to 1/2 inch thickness
4. Cut into biscuits
5. Bake 10-15 min
