## Ingredients
- 2 1/2 cup flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp ground cloves
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp nutmeg
- 1/2 tsp all spice
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 2 cups apple sauce
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup shortening
- 1 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup raisins
- 1/2 cup nuts, chopped


## Directions
1. Line a pan with parchment paper and grease both the pan and paper
2. Preheat oven to 280Â°F with pan inside
3. Sift dry ingredients together
4. Add wet ingredients and beat for 2 min
5. Bake for 45 min with cover on
