## Ingredients
- 160 gm all purpose flour
- 3 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 1/2 tablespoon baking powder
- Pinch of salt
- 180 gm sourdough discard
- 1 1/2 tablespoon oil
- 3/4 cup (180 gm) oat milk
- 1/2 cup applesauce
