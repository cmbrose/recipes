## Ingredients
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup margarine
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 cup sour cream

### Topping
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 6 Tbsp margarine
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 cup chopped nuts


## Directions
1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
2. Cream sugar and margarine
3. Add vanilla and eggs
4. Separately, mix dry ingredients
5. Alternatingly, add dry ingredients and sour cream to first mixture
6. Bake 50-60 min
