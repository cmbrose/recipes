## Ingredients
- 7 oz marshmallow cream
- 3 Tbsp water
- 2 cups powdered sugar
- 1/2 cup nuts
- 1 tsp vanilla


## Directions
1. Beat marshmallow cream, water, and vanilla on high speed until stiff peaks
2. Fold in powdered sugar and nuts
3. Spoon onto paper
