## Ingredients
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup oatmeal
- 1 cup coconut
- salt
- 2 1/2 cup flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 cup shortening
- 2 eggs


## Directions
1. Mix all ingredients except shortening and eggs together
2. Add melt shortening and lightly beat eggs, add to first mixture
3. Chill
4. Preheat oven to 375Â°F
5. Make into balls and flatten with a fork
6. Bake 12-15 min
