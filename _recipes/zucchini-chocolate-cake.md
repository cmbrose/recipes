## Ingredients
### Batter
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 1/4 cup butter
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 2 cups shredded zucchini
- 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 2 1/2 cup flour
- 1/2 cup cocoa
- 1/4 cup milk

### Icing
- 8 oz softened cream cheese
- 3 cups powdered sugar
- 1/2 Tbsp vanilla
- Touch of cocoa (optional)
