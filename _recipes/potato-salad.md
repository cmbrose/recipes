## Ingredients
- 3 lbs potatoes
- 5 eggs
- 2 cups mayo
- Mustard
- Sweet pickles
- Onion


## Directions
1. Peel and quarter potatoes
2. Boil potatoes with eggs
3. Cut up and mix potatoes/eggs, pickles, and onion
4. In a small bowl, mix mayo, pickle juice, and mustard
5. Pour mayo mixture over potato mixture
