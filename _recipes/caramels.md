## Ingredients
- 16 Tbps margarine
- 2 1/3 cup sugar
- 1 cup white syrup (karo)
- 1 can sweetened condensed milk
- 1 tsp vanilla


## Directions
1. Melt margarine
2. Add sugar, stirring only slightly, and bring to 320F
3. Stir in syrup
4. Slowly stir in milk and bring to 240F
5. Add vanilla
6. Pour into greased and lined 9x9 pan
7. Chill
