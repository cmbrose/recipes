## Ingredients
- 2 cups flour
- 1/4 cup cocoa
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1 cup salad dressing
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup hot water


## Directions
1. Sift dry ingredients together
2. Add dressing and vanilla and mix
3. Add hot water
