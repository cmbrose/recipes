## Ingredients
- 3 cups finely chopped rhubarb
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 cup Bisquick
- 1 1/2 cup sugar
- 3 Tbsp butter or oleo
- Salt
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup milk


## Directions
1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F
2. Grease 10" pie pan
3. Add rhubarb
4. In a mixer or blender, combine remaining ingredients
5. Pour mixture into pie pan
6. Bake 45-50 min
